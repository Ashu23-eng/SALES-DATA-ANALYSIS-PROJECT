## Recommendation
# According to the overall sales.
Increase stock and marketing before May (highest sales month).

# According to the beauty product.

Launch new collections before July (peak month).
# According to the clothing product.
Promote summer collections before May.
Introduce discounts or monsoon collections in July (low month).
# According to the electronic product.

# General Strategy
Increase inventory before high-demand months.
Use discounts and offers in low-demand months to boost sales
